What are Boopy mats?

MATERIAL

EVA mats are all-season and feature a unique surface of the material with diamond-shaped cells. Because of this, water does not spill when removing the carpet and does not splash when driving. The mats do not allow water to pass through.

DIFFERENCE

Rubber mats bend and climb on the pedals. EVA mats will not allow this! Special Velcro straps keep it in one position. If desired, a raised aluminum support is installed for the driver.

COLORING

EVA car mats can be ordered in individual sizes and in different colors, which are combined at the buyer's choice with the same colors of the edging polypropylene belt tape.
